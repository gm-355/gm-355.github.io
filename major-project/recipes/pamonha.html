Pamonha

Brazilian Sweet Corn Pudding

Time: 1h 30 min

Servings: 10

Ingredients

  • 20 ears of fresh corn
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/2 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 cups sugar

Instructions

  1. Carefully remove the corn husks and reserve. Cut the kernels off the cobs.
  2. Blend the kernels with the milk in a blender until smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture using a sieve and discard the pulp.
  4. Add the salt, condensed milk, melted butter, and sugar. Mix well.
  5. Wash the reserved husks. Sew or fold them into small “bags.”
  6. Pour about 1 cup of batter into each bag, tie with string, and place them into boiling water.
  7. Cook for about 1 hour. Drain and serve warm.
