Farofa

Toasted Cassava Flour Mix

Time: 20 min

Servings: 6-8

Ingredients

  • 100 ml vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 3 onions, finely chopped
  • 500 g toasted cassava flour
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil and butter together in a large pan.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté over medium heat until golden.
  3. Stir in the toasted cassava flour.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper.
  5. Cook, stirring constantly, for about 7 minutes.
  6. Turn off the heat, cover the pan, and let it rest for a few minutes.
  7. Serve warm.
