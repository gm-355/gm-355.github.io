Bolinha de Queijo

Fried Cheese Balls

Time: 40 min

Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 250 g shredded mozzarella
  • 1 egg
  • 4 tbsp cornstarch
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine mozzarella, egg, cornstarch and salt.
  2. Mix until a moldable dough forms.
  3. Shape small balls and place them on a tray.
  4. Freeze for 30 minutes.
  5. Fry in hot oil until golden, then drain on paper towels.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy.
