Quindim

Time: 45 min

Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 12 egg yolks (reserve the whites for another recipe)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup grated coconut (fresh or desiccated)
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • Butter and sugar for greasing the mold

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 °C (350 °F). Grease a quindim mold or a small baking pan with butter and sprinkle sugar evenly.
  2. In a large bowl, gently mix the egg yolks (without whisking too hard), sugar, grated coconut, melted butter and a pinch of salt until well combined.
  3. Pour the mixture into the prepared mold/pan.
  4. Place the mold inside a larger baking tray. Fill the tray with hot water (water bath / “banho-Maria”), about halfway up the mold height.
  5. Bake in the oven for about 40–50 minutes, or until the top is firm and golden.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool completely. Then refrigerate for at least 2 hours before unmolding.
  7. Once cold, invert onto a plate to serve. The quindim should come out shiny and firm.
