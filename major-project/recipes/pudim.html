Pudim

Brazilian Flan

Time: 1h 20 min

Servings: 10

Ingredients

  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar (for caramel)

Instructions

  1. Heat the sugar in a pan over medium heat until it melts and becomes caramel. Pour into a round mold and spread it around.
  2. Blend the condensed milk, regular milk, and eggs until smooth.
  3. Pour the mixture into the caramel-coated mold.
  4. Cover with foil and bake in a water bath at 350°F (180°C) for about 1 hour.
  5. Let it cool completely, refrigerate, and unmold.
