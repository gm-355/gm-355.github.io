Pão de Queijo

Time: 1 hour

Servings: ~40 pieces

Ingredients

  • 500 g sweet tapioca starch
  • 1 or 2 eggs
  • 250 ml whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp salt (level)
  • 1 packet grated Parmesan cheese
  • 350 g grated semi-cured cheese or mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk and oil in a pot until it just begins to boil. Remove from heat immediately.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the tapioca starch and salt. Pour the hot milk mixture over it and stir well.
  3. Add the Parmesan cheese, part of the grated cheese, and one egg. Mix with a spoon, then with your hands.
  4. Add the rest of the cheese. Check the dough texture — it should not be too dry or oily.
  5. If the dough feels dry, add one more egg. If too oily, add a bit more starch.
  6. The dough should release from your hands and from the bowl.
  7. Taste the dough and adjust salt if needed.
  8. Shape small balls and place them on a baking sheet, leaving space between them.
  9. No need to grease the baking sheet.
  10. Bake at 230°C (medium-high heat) until slightly golden.
  11. Tip: leftover pão de queijo can be toasted the next day.
