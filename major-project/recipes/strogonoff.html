Chicken Strogonoff

Time: 1 hour

Servings: 10

Ingredients

  • 3 chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup mustard
  • 1 cup mushrooms
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Potato sticks for serving

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the diced chicken with garlic, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter and sauté the onion until golden.
  3. Add the seasoned chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in the mushrooms, ketchup, and mustard.
  5. Add the heavy cream and remove from heat before it starts boiling.
  6. Serve with white rice and potato sticks.
