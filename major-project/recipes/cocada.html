Cocada

Coconut Candy Clusters

Time: 25 min

Servings: 10

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated coconut
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. In a pan, combine coconut, sugar, butter and condensed milk.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens slightly.
  3. When the mixture starts releasing from the bottom, turn off the heat.
  4. Let it cool a little, then shape portions using a spoon.
  5. Place them on a plate and let cool completely to firm up.
