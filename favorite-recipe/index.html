Tasty brazilian carrot cake. The carrot gives it a light, sweet flavor that goes perfectly with the rich chocolate on top.

Ingredients

  • Cake Ingredients
    • 3/4 cup oil
    • 1 1/2 cups sugar
    • 3 carrots
    • 3 eggs
    • 3 cups sifted all-purpose flour
    • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Frosting Ingredients ("Brigadeiro")
    • 1 can of condensed milk
    • 1/2 chocolate bar (3.5 oz). Choose 70% if you don't like it very sweet, and add more if you want it extra rich.
    • 1 cup of milk
    • 1 tbsp butter

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 356Â°F.
  2. If you have a bundt pan, the cake will look even prettier. Just grease it with oil, dust with flour until fully coated, and shake out the excess.
  3. Prepare the cake:
    1. Blend the oil and sugar well in a blender.
    2. Add chopped carrots and blend until smooth.
    3. Add the eggs one at a time, blending after each one.
    4. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and gently mix in the flour using a spatula.
    5. Add the baking powder and mix gently again.
    6. Pour the batter into a baking pan lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 40-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  5. Prepare the frosting:
    1. Add the condensed milk, chocolate, milk, and butter to a nonstick pan and stir over medium heat.
    2. When it starts to boil, lower the heat.
    3. The topping is ready when you gently tilt or shake the pan and the mixture completely pulls away from the bottom.
