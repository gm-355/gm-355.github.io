Carrot Cake

Prep time

1 hour

Yields

2–3 servings

Reviews

4 ★★★★☆ (1705)

Ingredients

  • Cake Ingredients
    • 3/4 cup oil
    • 1 1/2 cups sugar
    • 3 carrots
    • 3 eggs
    • 3 cups sifted all-purpose flour
    • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Frosting Ingredients ("Brigadeiro")
    • 1 can of condensed milk
    • 1/2 chocolate bar (70%)
    • 1 cup of milk
    • 1 tbsp butter

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 356°F.
  2. Grease the bundt pan with oil and dust with flour.
  3. Prepare the cake:
    1. Blend oil and sugar.
    2. Add carrots and blend until smooth.
    3. Add eggs one by one.
    4. Mix in flour gently with spatula.
    5. Add baking powder.
    6. Pour into pan lined with parchment paper.
  4. Bake for 40–50 minutes or until done.
  5. Prepare the frosting:
    1. Add ingredients to a nonstick pan and stir.
    2. Boil, then lower heat.
    3. Stop when mixture pulls from the bottom.
